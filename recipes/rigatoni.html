Rigatoni Recipe

Description

This Rigatoni recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Rigatoni noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the Rigatoni according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the Rigatoni rest before serving.
